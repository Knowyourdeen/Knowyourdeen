Section 01

Who We Are

GamHub Jobs is a CV generation and job matching platform designed for professionals in The Gambia. Our platform helps job seekers build professional CVs and cover letters, and connects them with employers across all industries in The Gambia.

We operate under Gambian law. Our data infrastructure uses Supabase, with servers located in the European Union (West EU region), subject to GDPR-equivalent data protection standards.

✉

Data Controller

[email protected]
Section 02

What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

Account and Identity Data

  • Email address (used as your account identifier via Supabase Auth magic link)
  • Name, professional title, and profile photo (provided voluntarily when building your CV)

CV and Professional Data

  • Full name, contact details (phone number, city, country)
  • Work history: job titles, employers, dates, and role descriptions
  • Educational background: institutions, qualifications, and years
  • Skills, certifications, languages, and professional achievements
  • Professional summary and cover letter content
  • References (names and contact details you choose to provide)

Employer Data

  • Company name, industry, and contact email
  • Job listing content including title, description, location, salary, and deadline
  • Subscription plan selected (Free, Featured, or Premium)

Usage Data

  • Number of PDF downloads used (stored in your Supabase profile to prevent abuse)
  • Saved job listings (stored in your browser's local storage)
  • Design preferences: colour palette, font choice, and CV theme (stored locally)

Payment Data

  • We do not store your payment card or mobile money details. All payment processing is handled entirely by ModemPay. We receive only a confirmation of payment success or failure.
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service: generating your CV, cover letter, and job matches
  • To authenticate you: managing your login session securely via Supabase Auth
  • To process payments: passing your name and email to ModemPay when you choose a paid plan
  • To prevent abuse: tracking your free download count server-side so it cannot be circumvented
  • To improve the platform: understanding which features are most used (we do not sell this data)
  • To publish job listings: displaying employer-submitted job posts to job seekers after admin review
We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your CV content, email address, or any personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
Section 04

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in two places:

  • Supabase (cloud database): Your account profile, email address, free download count, and submitted job listings are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase in the European Union (West EU region). Supabase applies industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Your browser (local storage): Your CV content, design choices, saved jobs, and wizard settings are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly use a feature that sends it to our servers (such as submitting a job post or logging in).

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for all connections, Row Level Security (RLS) on our database so users can only access their own records, and no storage of payment credentials on our servers.

As our servers are located in the European Union, your data may be processed outside The Gambia. We ensure this is done in compliance with applicable data protection principles.

Section 05

Payments and Financial Data

Payments on GamHub Jobs are processed by ModemPay, a Gambian payment gateway that supports mobile money and local payment methods in GMD (Gambian Dalasi).

  • We do not collect, process, or store your payment card numbers, mobile money PIN, or wallet credentials at any point.
  • When you initiate a payment, you are redirected to ModemPay's secure hosted checkout page. Your payment is processed entirely on their infrastructure.
  • After payment is confirmed, ModemPay notifies us of the transaction result only. We store a record that payment was made (amount, plan, timestamp) for service fulfilment purposes.
  • ModemPay's own privacy policy governs how your financial data is handled during checkout.
All transactions are denominated in Gambian Dalasi (GMD). GamHub Jobs does not process payments in any foreign currency.
Section 06

Cookies and Local Storage

GamHub Jobs uses your browser's local storage (not traditional cookies) to save your work between sessions. This is essential for the platform to function — without it, your CV data would be lost every time you closed the browser tab.

The data stored locally on your device includes:

  • gamhubjobs_cv_data — your CV content (name, experience, skills, etc.)
  • gamhubjobs_cv_wizard — your design choices (palette, font, profession)
  • gamhubjobs_cv_theme — your selected CV theme
  • folio_saved_jobs — jobs you have swiped right on
  • folio_gamhubjobs_cache — a 2-hour cache of job listings to reduce network requests
  • folio_cookie_consent — records whether you have accepted this notice

Supabase Auth also stores a session token in local storage to keep you logged in between visits. This token is encrypted and tied to your account only.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time through your browser's developer tools or settings. This will not affect your Supabase account data.

Section 07

Third-Party Services

GamHub Jobs uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — Database, authentication, and Edge Functions hosting. EU-based servers.
  • ModemPay (modem-pay.com) — Payment processing in GMD. Gambia-based payment provider.
  • GamHubJobs.com — We display publicly available job listings sourced from GamHubJobs. We do not share your data with GamHubJobs.
  • Google Fonts — Typography loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log your IP address when fonts are loaded.
  • html2pdf.js / Cloudflare CDN — PDF generation library loaded from Cloudflare's CDN.
Section 08

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can update or correct your information at any time by editing your CV or profile.
  • Right to erasure: You can request that we delete your account and all associated data. Email us at [email protected] to make this request.
  • Right to data portability: Your CV data is yours. You can download it as a PDF at any time.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can delete your locally stored data through your browser settings at any time.
  • Right to object: If you believe your data is being processed unlawfully, you may contact us or lodge a complaint with the relevant authority in The Gambia.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 14 business days.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

GamHub Jobs is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at [email protected] and we will delete the information promptly.
